Hypertension

OVERVIEW

Hypertension (high blood pressure) is
when the pressure in your blood vessel is too high (140/90mmHg or higher). It is common but can be serious if not treated. People with high blood pressure may not feel symptoms.The only way to know is to get your blood pressure checked. Things that increase the risk of having high blood pressure include: Older age,Genetic being overweight or obese, not being physically active, high-salt diet and drinking too much alcohol. lifesytle changes like eating a healthier diet, quitting tobacco and being more active can help lower blood pressure.Some people may still need to take medicines.
